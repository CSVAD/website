  • Review of Parametric Forms (30 min)

    In Groups of 2, experiment with a partner's parametric model for 7 minutes. Save 3 screenshots of variations you create with their interface.

    Engage in a short discussion with your partner (7 minutes). Compare your results with the variations they created with their own system. How similar or different are they? How do the results created by another person conform with or deviate from the constraints you embedded in your original design?

    • Follow with a joint group review where volunteers will share their results.
